Common questions

Got Questions?

What purity of gold is accepted?+
Most lenders accept gold between 18 and 24 karats. Purity is assessed at the branch using certified assaying equipment. Gold coins issued by banks are also accepted.
How is the gold loan amount calculated?+
Lenders offer up to 75% of the gold's market value (LTV). The rate per gram is based on the 30-day average gold price. RUPYA compares multiple lenders to get you the best rate per gram.
Is my gold safe with the lender?+
Absolutely. Your gold is stored in bank-grade vaults with 24x7 security, CCTV monitoring, and full insurance coverage. You get a receipt for every gram pledged.
Can I repay the loan before the tenure?+
Yes. Most gold loans allow full or partial pre-closure without any penalty. You can also pay interest only and repay the principal at the end of the tenure (Bullet repayment).
What happens if I can't repay my gold loan?+
The lender will give you notice and may auction your gold to recover the outstanding amount. RUPYA always advises borrowers to inform the lender proactively if they anticipate repayment difficulties.

Common questions

Got Questions?

What documents are required for a business loan?+
Typically: PAN and Aadhaar of all directors, business registration, last 2 years ITR with financials, bank statements for 12 months, and GST returns if applicable. RUPYA's team helps you organise these.
What is the minimum business vintage required?+
Most lenders require a business to be operational for at least 1–2 years. Some NBFCs offer new business loans for businesses under 1 year with stronger personal credit profiles.
Can a startup with no profits get a business loan?+
Some NBFCs offer revenue-based financing and startup loans where EBITDA is less relevant. Strong GST turnover and bank transactions help. RUPYA can match you with lenders open to your profile.
Is GST registration mandatory?+
For many lenders, yes — especially for loans above ₹25 lakh. However, some NBFCs lend to businesses below the GST threshold. Use our eligibility checker to see what you qualify for.
How is business loan interest calculated?+
Business loans typically use a flat or reducing balance method. RUPYA's EMI calculator shows you the exact monthly outflow and total interest cost for any rate and tenure combination.

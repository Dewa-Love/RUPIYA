Common questions

Got Questions?

How long does approval take?+
Most personal loan applications processed through RUPYA receive in-principle approval within 5–10 minutes. Final disbursement after document verification typically takes 4–24 hours, depending on the lender.
Will applying affect my CIBIL score?+
Checking your eligibility on RUPYA is a soft inquiry and does NOT affect your credit score. Your score is only impacted when a lender makes a hard inquiry after you formally apply.
Can I apply if I'm self-employed?+
Yes! Many of our 50+ partner lenders offer personal loans to self-employed individuals and business owners. You'll need to provide your ITR for the last 2 years and business financials.
What is the minimum credit score required?+
Most lenders require a CIBIL score of 650+. However, some NBFCs on our platform have more flexible criteria. Use our Eligibility Checker to see which lenders you qualify for.
Is there a prepayment penalty?+
Varies by lender. Many of our partner banks offer zero prepayment charges after 6–12 months. We clearly show prepayment terms for every offer before you apply.

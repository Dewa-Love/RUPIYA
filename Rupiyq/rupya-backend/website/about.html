2021 — The Beginning

Born from Frustration

Our founders spent months trying to get a home loan after relocating to Mumbai. Every bank gave a different rate. Every agent had a hidden fee. RUPYA was launched with 5 lender partners and a simple promise: zero platform fee, always. The first 100 borrowers were funded in under 30 days.

5
Founding Lender Partners
2022 — First Milestone

₹1,000 Crore Milestone

Crossed ₹1,000 Cr in loans facilitated within 14 months of launch. Expanded to 12 cities across India. Launched the EMI calculator and basic eligibility tool. Grew the team from 8 to 40 people. Won Best Fintech Startup at the ET Startup Awards.

Loans Facilitated
2023 — Series A

$8M Raised

Raised $8M from Sequoia India and Accel Partners. Used the capital to build our proprietary eligibility engine that matches borrowers to lenders in under 60 seconds. Expanded to Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. Launched the mobile app. Crossed 50,000 borrowers.

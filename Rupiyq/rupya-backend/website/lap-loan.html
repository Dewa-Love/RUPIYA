Common questions

Got Questions?

How is the loan amount calculated?+
Most lenders offer 50–70% of the property's current market value (LTV). RUPYA works with independent valuers who ensure you get a fair, current valuation.
Can I pledge a property in my spouse's name?+
Yes, the property owner can be a co-applicant or guarantor. Many lenders accept joint ownership properties.
What if my property already has an existing home loan?+
You can still apply for LAP on properties with an existing home loan, subject to available equity. The existing loan must be serviced regularly.
How long does LAP approval take?+
Unlike personal loans, LAP requires property valuation and legal verification. The typical timeline is 5–10 business days. RUPYA's lender partners have streamlined this process.
What is the minimum credit score for LAP?+
Typically 650+. However, since the loan is secured, some lenders are more flexible with credit score requirements compared to unsecured loans.

Common questions

Got Questions?

What is the LTV ratio for car loans?+
For new cars, most lenders offer up to 85–100% of the on-road price. For pre-owned cars, typically 75–85% of the market value assessed by the lender's evaluator.
Can I get a car loan without income proof?+
It's challenging, but some lenders accept bank statements in lieu of income proof. Co-applicants and strong CIBIL scores also help in such cases.
What vehicles are eligible?+
Passenger cars, SUVs, MUVs from all manufacturers. Commercial vehicles, two-wheelers and electric vehicles are covered under separate loan categories.
How long does it take to get a car loan?+
RUPYA can get you pre-approved in minutes. Final disbursement after document verification typically takes 4–24 hours, depending on the dealership and lender.
What happens if I miss a car loan EMI?+
Missing an EMI attracts a late payment fee and negatively impacts your CIBIL score. Consecutive defaults can result in the lender initiating vehicle repossession. Always call your lender proactively if you foresee difficulty.
